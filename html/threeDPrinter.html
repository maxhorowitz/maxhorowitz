3D Printer

During 11th Grade, I built a do-it-yourself Prusa i3 3D printer. This project gave me acute insight into the works of computer-numerically-controlled machines and 3D printing. Since then, I have become very proficient in computer-aided-design, both 2D and 3D. I use software such as Solidworks, Autodesk Fusion 360, Repetier Host, Ultimaker Cura, and Slicer to produce readable ".gcode" files that can be made on my 3D printer. Since assembling it, I have had an array of practical uses and projects which require 3D printing technology.
